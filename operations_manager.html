Growth Operations Marketing Manager (Non-profit)

  1. (Full-Time, Seattle WA)

Role:

You’re a misfit, a marketer, a jack-of-all-trades, who possesses high resourcefulness and the ability to recruit and manage others towards a common goal. You should also have a strong bias towards action.

As an Operations Manager in Marketing, you are fundamental to our success. You would see projects through from conception to implementation. You are a strong multi-tasker who thrives in a fast paced-environment.

Marketing, project management, people management, customer relationship, user on-boarding all sit under the Operation Manager's broad umbrella of responsibilities. This is where marketing meets operational execution head on.

WHAT YOU'LL DO:

• Oversee daily operations of the company
• Collect project requirements thoroughly in spite of great level of uncertainty
• Manage relationships with partners and 3rd party vendors
• Be the frontline for all of our customer relationships
• Wrangle Data to improve the accuracy of your work
• Develop relationships with new users, partners, clients to learn tidbits below the surface

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR:

• Humbleness
• Some level of familiarity across various marketing functions, including: media buying, retargeting, creative design, video editing, copywriting
• Familiarity with at least one ad platform (Facebook, AdRoll, Google, etc)
• Experience managing and recruiting freelancers across
Fiverr, Upwork, and other on-demand hiring platforms
• Prior founder-level experience a big plus
• Understanding of business functions such as Marketing, Sales Development, Event Planning
• Outstanding organizational and leadership abilities
• Working knowledge of Data applications and SQL - a plus

REQUIRED SKILLS:

  • • Be driven to accomplish a lot and learn a LOT very quickly
  • • Ability to prioritize tasks and track progress
  • • Ability to work with outside contractors and other employees
  • • Able to effectively give and receive constructive feedback
  • • Must have a no-excuse, results-focused mindset

PERKS:

• Be the 1st full-time hire we make for this team
• Have the credibility behind your back to tell friends/family that you are part of a truly mission-driven start-up (we are both a non-profit & a for-profit organization)
