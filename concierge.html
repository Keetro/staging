Digital Concierge Ninja - Part-time / Remote

  1. (Contract, Remote)
At Keetro our mission is to promote professional person-to-person conversations. As a society, we never faced a bigger social interaction gap as now during the COVID-19 epidemic. Professional communication has become a privelege. As a company, we've been on this mission for a long time, but there has never been as much urgency to make sure that the best ideas have a way to reach their audience.

Role:

As a Concierge Ninja, your role is to change the world one person at a time. For most job ads, this would be a complete BS, but in our case we truly believe this is what this role is about. And as a world changer, you will be fundamental to our success, helping us reinvent how people are connected around the world.

Every few days, you will be given a list of people who showed interest in one of our Webinars, Virtual Roundtables, Web Talks (e.g. "Initiatives"). Your job for the day will be to contact these people and welcome them to the next phase of their personal journey. You are successful when everyone you touch feels like “it is about them” - never about you or us.

During your job interview process, you will be asked to complete part of the job, including writing emails and making phone calls. If successful, you will have an option to continue working with us.

About You:

You’ve probably worked at one point in your life in Hospitality Industry (Restaurant, Hotel), but then started considering work in Recruiting or Sales. As a result, you are a strong multi-tasker who thrives in a fast paced-environment. And you are keen on embracing latest tactics–never afraid of the consequences–because that’s who you are: fearless. If something does not work out, you try again. And again. And again…
