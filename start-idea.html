Frequently Asked Questions

How big can my event be?

At this time, we focus only on small events (10-20 attendees).

Are there any restrictions on my attendees?

All your attendees need to be peers to one another.

Can I host a party?

This event has to be professionally-oriented.

Do I have to pay for my events?

Yes. In order to match you with professional event planners and marketers, we will require a payment.

Do I need to cover 100% of the event cost?

No. Depending on the event, the team you are matched with might be able to offer attendee tickets at a price point that covers part of the expense.
